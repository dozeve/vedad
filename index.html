Case study

CoffeeHouse — from visit to reservation

Goal: increase table reservations for a neighborhood café.

Approach: designed a clean hero with one CTA (“Book a table”), highlighted the menu and location, and reduced steps to submit.

Execution: performance-first layout, optimized images, lazy loading, and lightweight animations.

Form: frictionless contact using Web3Forms and a clear success state.

Outcome: faster page, clearer path, higher intent.

LCP ~0.9s (4G) +18–30% conversions* Lighthouse 95+/100

*Indicative uplift based on A/B on demo traffic.
